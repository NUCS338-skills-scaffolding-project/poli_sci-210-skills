AI Memos
POLI SCI 210 · Assignment Breakdown

At a Glance
Points

10 points each × 7 instances

Grading

Satisfactory / Unsatisfactory (full 10 points for satisfactory)

Length

500–1,000 words (plus transcript attachment)

Tool

Microsoft Copilot recommended; any generative AI chatbot acceptable

Release

No later than the first lecture of the corresponding week

Due

Fridays at 11:59 PM of the week assigned

Rubric

Posted on Canvas

Purpose
Students prompt a generative AI chatbot to explain a concept, topic, or issue related to the
corresponding week's theme, then evaluate the AI's output against the course material.

What the Syllabus Requires
Interaction phase:
•

Ask the AI to explain a concept, topic, or issue tied to the week's lecture and textbook material.

•

Ask at least five follow-up or clarification questions on the topic.

•

Be creative with prompts, as long as they align with the course material and the Canvas rubric.

Memo phase (500–1,000 words):
Summarize the interaction and evaluate its quality. The syllabus flags these evaluative questions:
•

How does the AI explanation compare to what we discussed in class?

•

Why is it different from the course material?

•

Why is it more or less helpful than what is covered in the textbook or lectures?

•

Do you feel the recommended sources (if any) are credible?

Transcript: Attach a transcript of the conversation (export to Word directly from the chat, include an
external URL, or submit a separate file).
